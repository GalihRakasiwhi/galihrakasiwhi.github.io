Benefit

Why Choosing Us?

It is located in a strategic elite area in Bandung, supported by easy access for them who live in the city or out of the city.

Alternative workspaces are available in various capacities which is suitable for private office, coworking, meeting room, classroom and function room.

An open working area, podcast studio, photo/ video studio, lounge room are available as well.

We provide sufficient internet connection which is easily access the international journals. Printer and scanner are also provided.

Other supporting facilities are reception room, large parking area, mushala, separate toilet and restroom for men and women, corner cafe.
